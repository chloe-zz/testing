The watch for playing life - VAGARY

VAGARY is a watch borned in Italy and designed to help people enjoy their lives with the concept of "Happy & Surprise", the brand has produced a watch to be worn by adults with a sense of fun whose lifestyles are inadequately expressed by conventional watches. Unconstrained form and coloring with an Italian sensibility infuses it with a sense of fun, not only for the wearer but also for to those simply looking at it. Why not express yourself with a watch from the world of VAGARY?
